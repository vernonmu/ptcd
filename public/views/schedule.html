Premier Track Club was established in 2006 by Track and Field coach, Charles Hinton. Charles "Squeaky" Hinton is a certified USA Track & Field Level One & Level Two Track and Field instructor, and has twenty four years of track coaching experience. Coach Hinton is a graduate of Galveston-Ball High School in Galveston, Texas where he was a two time state champion track runner. From there he attended Texas A&I and was named two time All American for his accomplishments in their track and field program. Coach Hinton coached two years at W.T.
