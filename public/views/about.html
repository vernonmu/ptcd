About

Premier Track Club is a competitive track and field club that participates in USA Track and Field, AAU, and TAAF track events at various locations within the Dallas Fort Worth Metroplex . We accept male and female applicants between the ages of eight and nineteen and we encourage parental participation. Although we are a competitive club, we accept all athletes and do not reject applicates based on performance or ability. We emphasize a solid, technical education in proper running technique for each race distance and build on these mechanics throughout the program's duration. Premier Track Club has competed in Dallas over the past ten years and we look forward to working with you and your athlete as we have fun breaking old and setting new personal records.

Club History

Premier Track Club was established in 2006 by Track and Field coach, Charles Hinton. Charles "Squeaky" Hinton is a certified USA Track & Field Level One & Level Two Track and Field instructor, and has twenty four years of track coaching experience. Coach Hinton is a graduate of Galveston-Ball High School in Galveston, Texas where he was a two time state champion track runner. From there he attended Texas A&I and was named two time All American for his accomplishments in their track and field program. Coach Hinton coached two years at W.T.
